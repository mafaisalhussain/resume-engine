# Fluff Word Blacklist

Never write any of the following words or phrases anywhere in a resume. Scan every bullet before finalizing and replace any match with a specific verb or concrete outcome.

## Banned Words

rigorous, systematic, robust, passionate, demonstrating, aligned with stakeholder, translating complex, accessible, effectively, leveraging, utilizing, showcasing, enabling, impactful, innovative, seamless, cutting-edge, transformative, actionable, state-of-the-art, best practices, deep dive, synergy, holistic, dynamic, results-driven, self-starter, team player, fast learner, detail-oriented, proactive, motivated, goal-oriented, hands-on, spearheaded, excited, unique opportunity

## Also Banned in Summaries

"passionate about", "team player", "fast learner", "excited to", "unique opportunity"

## Why This Matters

Each of these words is used by every candidate. They add length without adding evidence. Reviewers score D6 (Resume Clarity) down for each occurrence. Replace with the specific action, tool, or quantified result that the word was obscuring.
