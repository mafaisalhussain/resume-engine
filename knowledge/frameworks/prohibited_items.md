# Prohibited Items

The following must never appear in any generated, reviewed, or optimized resume. Treat any occurrence as an UNSUPPORTED claim that immediately fails the quality gate.

## Tools the Candidate Has Not Used

- **LangChain** — excluded permanently. LlamaIndex is theirs; LangChain is not.
- Do not substitute any other tool or framework not documented in profile.md or a projects/*.md file.

## Certifications to Drop

- **Forage virtual experience programs** — Accenture North America, Tata Group, and any other Forage simulation. These are 5-hour online exercises; recruiters recognize them and they carry zero weight.
- Keep: National Instruments LabVIEW certification, Entuple VLSI Design and Simulation.

## LaTeX / Formatting Violations

- **`$\sim$`** — the LaTeX math tilde (Unicode U+223C). It breaks ATS text parsing. Write "approximately X\%" or "up to X\%" instead.

## Metrics and Claims

- Any metric not directly traceable to profile.md or a projects/*.md file.
- Any tool, project, company, or date range not in the knowledge base.
- Invented responsibilities or scope of work.

## Hard-Coded Facts (Never Change)

- **UMBC graduation:** always `Expected December 2026`. Do not change this date.
- Short-tenure roles (3 months or less): verbs restricted to Built / Developed / Implemented / Configured / Deployed. Never use "Architected", "Spearheaded", or "Led" for intern/short-contract scope.
